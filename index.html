About Me

I'm currently a junior studying Clinical Neuroscience and Creative Writing at Virginia Tech. When I'm living others people's lives through stories or attempting to get into someone else's head with stories of my own you can find me in my kitchen forcing friends and family to become test guinea pigs for my food. Here, you'll find some (hopefully) interesting material on the mind and different places where it takes a central role, ranging from practicing productivity and empathy to mental health awareness.
